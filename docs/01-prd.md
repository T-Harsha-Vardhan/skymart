# SkyMart v1 --- PRD

# Vision

SkyMart aims to make online shopping simple, fast, and enjoyable through a clean interface, intuitive navigation, and a modern digital storefront that prioritizes product discovery and user experience.

## Product Goals

- Enable customers to discover products quickly.
- Provide a frictionless shopping experience.
- Make checkout intuitive and fast.
- Build a scalable frontend architecture that supports future product growth.

## Scope

Version 1 provides a complete customer shopping journey including account authentication, product discovery, product exploration, shopping cart management, and a simulated checkout experience.
